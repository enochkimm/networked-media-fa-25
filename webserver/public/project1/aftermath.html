Aftermath

I didn't understand what was going on at all, so I started searching on Google

After about an hour of researching and convincing myself that I was, in fact, dumb, I finally reached the conclusions that I got ratted

RATs (Remote Access Trojan) are essentially a malware that allows a person to take control of your PC

So, what did I do?
I downloaded a AntiVirus (don't buy these, these are scams) and tried removing it

It didn't do anything

So after another hour of research and randomly clicking in my Settings, I found my fix

I booted into Safe Mode, then ran a full scan with Windows Defender. Defender found a couple of suspicious files, quarantined them, and suggested a cleanup.

I removed the quarantined files, cleared temporary folders, and then booted normally. I changed passwords for my email and game accounts, and enabled two-factor authentication where possible. It was really tedious, but eventually things returned to normal.
